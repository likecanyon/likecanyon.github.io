Ziqi Gao

I'm a PhD Student at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences , where I conduct research about Robotics and Artificial Intelligence in State Key Laboratory of Robotcs, Shenyang Institue of Automation.

I am supervised by Prof. Yiwen Zhao Glass, Lens Blur, HDR+, VR, Portrait Mode, Portrait Light, and Maps. I did my PhD at UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Jitendra Malik. I've received the PAMI Young Researcher Award.
